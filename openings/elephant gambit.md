## Elephant Gambit Opening Lines

**Opening Description:**
The Elephant Gambit is an opening that starts with 1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 d5 3. exd5, where Black sacrifices a pawn for active piece play. It's a lesser-known but aggressive choice that leads to complex, tactical positions. The main line involves the pawn sacrifice with ...e4, leading to dynamic middlegame positions.
